Frequently Asked Questions!

Question 1 : What payment methods are accepted on your online frontend florist app?

Answer: We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and PayPal.

Question 2 : How do I track my order?

Answer: You can track your order by logging into your account or contacting us.

Question 3: How do I place an order?

Answer:simply browse through our selection of bouquets,choose the one you like, and click on Add to Cart button.

Question 4: How can I track my order after I have placed it?

Answer: Once your order is confirmed, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number that you can use to track the status of your delivery.

Question 5: Do you offer same-day delivery for orders placed?

Answer: Yes, we offer same-day delivery for orders placed before 1:00pm within Auckland.

Question 6: Can I schedule a bouquet delivery for a future date ?

Answer: Yes. you can schedule a bouquet delivery by filling in the ContactUs form and we will reply to you.

Question 7: Do you offer a subscription service for regular flower deliveries?

Answer: Yes, we offer a subscription service for customers who want to receive regular flower deliveries on a weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly basis. Simply sign up for our subscription service on our app and customize your delivery preferences to suit your needs.
